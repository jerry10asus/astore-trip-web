About AStore Trip

AStore Trip is a travel-inspired app shaped by design and architecture. It brings together Apple Store locations around the world so you can explore each city’s stores, save favorites, check in, and appreciate their unique design.

 Store Search

Quickly find Apple Stores worldwide, with architecture details and map locations.

❤️ Favorites

Build your Apple Store list and remember every stop on your journey.

🏅 Check-ins & Achievements

Check in after visiting a store and collect badges on your design-inspired travels.
